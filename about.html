About Me

I was first exposed to 3D printing in high school and I thought it was fascinating but too expensive and complicated to seriously consider as a hobby. I have a background in Computer Aided Design (CAD) and it was through my love of CAD that I finally convinced myself to invest in a 3D printer in order to bring my creations into the real world.
