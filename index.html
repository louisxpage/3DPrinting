My name is Louis Page and this website was made to document my adventures in 3D printing.


The printer I use is called the Prusa i3 MK3. It is an FDM type 3D Printer. This means that it uses a hot extruder to extrude plastic special thrermoplastic, refered to as filament, onto the print bed along its X, Y, and Z axis.
