These are some resources that I have found helpful while 3D printing


Prusa Research

Designed by Josef Prusa, the Prusa I3 MK3 is a great entry level 3D printer that offers high performace at a reasonable cost.


Thingiverse

Thingiverse is one of the main 3D file sharing sites that is designed to share 3D printing files among creators.


MyMiniFactory

MyMiniFactory is similar to Thingiverse but it allows you to upload your own designs and sell them on the marketplace.

Fusion 360

Autodesk Fusion 360 is a CAD software that is easy to use and has a wide variety of functionality. It is the most popular CAD software in the 3D printing community.
